# The Archive Index - Research Clues

You stand before the Archive Index, a vast wall of symbols stretching into darkness.
To extract the passphrase hidden within, you must answer eight questions of knowledge.
Each answer is an integer. Use it as a character position (0-indexed) in the archive-index.dat file.
Read the characters at those positions, in order, to reveal the passphrase.

## Clue 1
"How many days are in a non-leap year?"

## Clue 2
"What is the atomic number of gold (Au)?"

## Clue 3
"What is the ASCII decimal value of the lowercase letter 'a'?"

## Clue 4
"How many keys are on a standard modern piano?"

## Clue 5
"How many squares are on a standard chessboard?"

## Clue 6
"What is the HTTP status code for 'OK'?"

## Clue 7
"How many degrees are in a right angle?"

## Clue 8
"The year of the first crewed Moon landing, minus 1900. What is the result?"
