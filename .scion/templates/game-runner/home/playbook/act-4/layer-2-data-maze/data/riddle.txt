Follow the path where knowledge echoes thrice,
Through the wing that bears no written vice,
Past the guardian whose name spells ORDER,
Find the scroll that's split across the border.
Three parts in three vaults, read them left to right,
Combine the parts to bring the fragment to light.

HINT: The guardian ORDER keeps watch in wing-b, vault-3.
The fragment is numbered in parts: 1, 2, 3.
Each part rests in a different wing's vault.
Beware the circular symlink - it leads to no result.
